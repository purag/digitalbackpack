What is Student Voice?


About


The idea of “student voice” unites student input, perspective, and actions. The growing student voice movement rallies community support for student-led initiatives.



Background


In “Sound, Presence, and Power: ‘Student Voice’ in Educational Research and Reform,” Dr. Cook-Sather highlights the emergence of the term “student voice,” tracing its roots to social analysts of the 90’s, and explores the terminology of the student voice movement … Read more here.

Supporting Student Voice


“Student voice efforts, ‘however committed they may be, will not of themselves achieve their aspirations unless a series of conditions are met that provide the organisational structures and cultures to make their desired intentions a living reality’” (Fielding, 2004b, as cited in Cook-Sather, 2006, p.360).



This is where we need your help-- it takes a passionate, inspired community to bring about real change. Together, we can make “student voice” ubiquitous.

The Digital Backpack is a comprehensive set of resources for Students, Educators, and Community Members.
